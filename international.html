WHY CHOOSE STAR?

Every year, students from around the world choose to study at Star College. Why Star? As an applied academic institution, Star College combines the academic foundations of a university with the employer-ready skills of a college. At Star, you will receive the best of both worlds: a deep, well-rounded education and the applied, real-world skills necessary to achieve academic and career success.

WHAT'S THE STAR DIFFERENCE?

  • Programs that are practical and applied
  • Smaller class sizes
  • Access to a wide variety of career opportunities
  • A strong return-on-investment
  • A unique campus experience
  • The opportunity to stay globally-engaged

WHEN TO APPLY - OPEN ENROLMENT PROGRAM

Students are encouraged to apply early. Applications are accepted up to one year prior to the semester starting

  • Fall Semester: Applications open early October of the previous year
  • Winter Semester: Applications open early February of the previous year
  • Summer Semester: Applications open early June of the previous year
