ACADEMIC ADVISING

Star College’s experienced academic advisors assist you with program and course selection prior to and during each semester’s registration. They can also assist you with personal issues, career planning and university selection. Typically, a student meets with an academic advisor on a one-to-one basis and a study plan is worked out. An academic advisor will help you to complete a course planning form in which the courses appropriate to your intended major are filled in and an appointment date set for registration.

LEARNING CENTER

The Learning Centre offers assistance to students in their coursework and beyond, including individual tutoring sessions in English, Mathematics, Economics, and Social Sciences.
Students can book one to two free 30-minute tutoring appointments per week. If you have any questions or would like to visit the tutors, you can book an appointment and it is available on zoom.

CAREER SERVICES

Career Services will provide the following services:

  • Resume and cover letter improvement
  • Host career and employment-themed workshops (networking, enhancing interviewing skills, job search techniques etc.)
  • Inviting employers from various industries to connect with students
  • Conduct mock interviews to help prepare students for the job market
  • Connect students to resources to enhance job search strategies
  • Create and find networking opportunities for students
